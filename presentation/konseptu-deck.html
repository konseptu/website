Product · Customer Feedback
revue Customer Feedback System

Understand your customer

Revue captures how customers really feel right at the point of service, in just a tap or two. Friction low enough that you hear from everyone, not only the complainers, with a form you shape as simple or as detailed as you want.

Build the form your way. As shallow or as granular as you like, a smiley-face scale, repeatable thumbs up or down, or nested tag questions, and change it anytime without a developer.
See it all on a live dashboard. Every response lands instantly, with ratings, trends, and reports you can filter and export, and it keeps working even when you're offline.
Fits how you serve. Bring it to the table, set it at the counter, or station it by the exit, wherever earns the most honest responses.
Make every rating specific. When you want, staff can log what a customer actually received, so they rate their real order rather than a vague overall score.
